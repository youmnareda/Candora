Our Origin

A Small Studio.
A Big Obsession.

Candora began in 2019 in a 400-square-foot Brooklyn studio, where founder Lena Ashford spent months experimenting with soy wax ratios, botanical oil blends, and hand-thrown ceramic forms. The goal was never just a candle — it was an atmosphere.

Frustrated by products that burned fast and smelled synthetic, Lena set a personal rule: every Candora candle had to last at least 60 hours and use only therapeutic-grade essential oils. No shortcuts. No fillers.

Today, that same obsession guides a team of twelve artisans who hand-pour every vessel in small batches, ensuring each one carries the care and intention of the first.
